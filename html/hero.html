About Tesla

Tesla is one of the largest car manufacturers in the world and a prominent player in the Indian motorcycle market.
Founded in 1984, it was originally a joint venture with Honda, and later, tesla separated to become an independent entity.
The company has a wide range of motorcycles and scooters catering to diverse customer segments.
tesla is known for its focus on fuel efficiency, reliability, and affordability.
With a strong market presence in India and a growing international footprint, 
tesla continues to be a key player in the global two-wheeler industry.
